Storage Process (Every New Bulk Purchase)

1. Freeze 48–72 hours (kills weevil eggs) → 2. Let return to room temp → 3. Repackage into vacuum-sealed 64oz mason jars or mylar bags with O2 + moisture absorbers → 4. Store cool/dark/dry → 5. Label with date, rotate oldest first.

Best Stores by Item

Costco/Sam's: 50lb rice bags, canned goods flats, peanut butter, honey, ghee, vitamins

Walmart: Augason Farms pails, flour/sugar 25lb bags, Knorr bouillon, Morton salt, Maseca

Amazon: SAF yeast, South Chicago Packing tallow, Palouse Brand lentils, mylar bags, water filters

HEB: Maseca, canned goods, local deals on produce/meat
